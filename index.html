Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball team sport played between two teams of 11 players each on an oval field, featuring
a central 22-yard pitch with wickets at both ends

Gameplay: Two batters are on the field at a time.
The bowler delivers six balls (an "over") towards the wicket,
aiming to get the batter out
(e.g., bowled, caught, leg before wicket).

football

the world's most popular sport, featuring two teams of 11 players aiming to
score by maneuvering a ball into the opponent's goal,
primarily using their feet.

Key Rules
Players cannot use hands or arms,
except for goalkeepers within their own penalty area.
A match consists of two 45-minute halves.

Essential Skills:Cricket Football, requires skills like dribbling,
passing, shooting, and heading.

Objective: To outscore the opponent; if both teams score the same, it is a draw, or it goes to extra time/penalty shoot-out.

Origins: Modern football began in 19th-century Britain,,
but early versions were recorded inChina (Tsu' Chu) as early as the third century BC.
